# Financial-Performance-Dashboard-Power-BI-
## Project Overview

This project presents a Financial Performance Dashboard built in Power BI to analyse revenue, profitability, and product performance across different markets and customer segments.

The dashboard is designed to simulate real-world financial reporting used by business and finance teams, enabling users to quickly understand business performance and identify key drivers of revenue and profit.

The report consists of two interactive pages:

#### Executive Summary Dashboard

#### Financial Deep Dive Analysis

Together, these pages provide both high-level KPIs and detailed analytical insights.

## Dashboard Preview
#### Executive Summary

Key metrics displayed:

Total Revenue

Total Profit

Profit Margin

Total Units Sold

The executive view provides a quick overview of business performance and highlights key financial trends.

#### Financial Deep Dive Analysis

This page provides detailed insights into revenue contribution, product profitability, and market performance.


## Business Questions Answered

#### This dashboard helps answer several important financial and business questions:

How does revenue change over time?

Which countries generate the most revenue?

Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?

Which products generate the highest profit?

Which products have the highest profit margins?

Which markets have high revenue but lower profitability?


## Key Insights

The United States generates the highest revenue among all markets.

The Government and Small Business segments contribute the largest share of revenue.

Amarilla has the highest profit margin (~16%), indicating strong profitability.

Paseo generates the highest total profit among all products.

Revenue peaks around October–November, suggesting potential seasonal demand


## Dashboard Features

The report includes several interactive elements:

Country filter

Segment filter

Interactive charts that update dynamically based on selected filters

This allows users to perform dynamic financial analysis across markets and customer groups.


## Tools & Technologies Used

Power BI

Data modelling using DAX measures

Data visualization and dashboard design

Business performance analysis


## Key Metrics Calculated

The dashboard includes several financial KPIs created using DAX:

Total Revenue

Total Profit

Profit Margin

Total Units Sold

Profit per Unit

These metrics allow deeper analysis of business profitability and efficiency.
